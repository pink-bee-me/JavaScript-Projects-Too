Rules of The Game:

It's quite simple...

  • First: You will click a button and a clip from a music video will be played for you. (Thank You YouTube.com !!!)

  • Second: After viewing the video clip, you will be asked if you can NAME THAT TUNE !!!

  • Third: You will input your best answer and press enter to see if you can, indeed, NAME THAT TUNE!!!

  • Finally: The Game will let us know if you were correct when you "Named That Tune" !!! Oh yeah !!! Fun Fun !!!
