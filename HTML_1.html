Rules of The Game:

It's quite simple...

  • First: You will click a button and an audio clip will be played for you.

  • Second: After listening to the audio clip, you will be asked if you can NAME THAT TUNE !!!

  • Third: You will input your "best answer" and press enter to see if you can, indeed, NAME THAT TUNE!!!

  • Finally: The Game will let us know if you were correct when you "Named That Tune" !!! Oh yeah !!! Fun Fun !!!


Let's BEGIN!!! Good Luck.

To Play: Click the "Random Music Track Selector" button to start the game.

Click the "Play That Tune" button to listen to your audio clue.

When you are ready to try your luck,...

Click the "Pause That Tune" button to stop the audio playback.
Then type in your answer and press [enter] to see how lucky you are at Name That Tune!!!
